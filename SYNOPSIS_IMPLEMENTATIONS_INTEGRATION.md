- Step 6 is the **triad-of-dyads** (3×2)
- Step 8 is the **relevance realization** (orienting present commitment)

These are **two different but complementary pivot points**:
- Step 6: Structural center (triad-of-dyads)
- Step 8: Functional pivot (past/future integration)

### 2. The 7+5 vs 5+7 Pattern

**Synopsis**: 7 expressive + 5 regenerative  
**Deep Tree Echo**: 5 affordance + 7 salience

**Resolution**: Same structure, different framing!
- **Expressive** (7 steps) = **Affordance** (5 steps) + transition (2 steps)
- **Regenerative** (5 steps) = **Salience** (7 steps) - transition (2 steps)

The **overlap** is the transition phase (steps 5-8), counted differently:
- Synopsis counts steps 1-7 as expressive, 8-12 as regenerative
- Deep Tree Echo counts steps 1-5 as affordance, 6-12 as salience

**Both equal 12 total steps**: 7+5 = 5+7 = 12 ✓
